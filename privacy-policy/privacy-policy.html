Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of Ecoloader ("we," "us," "our") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of email addresses gathered from users ("you") of our platform.

Information Collection

Types of Information Collected: We collect only email addresses.

Method of Collection: Email addresses are collected when users voluntarily provide them during the usage of our platform or through direct communication with us.

Use of Information

Purpose: The collected email addresses are used solely for communicating with users about our services, updates, and relevant notifications.

No Further Processing: We do not use email addresses for any other purpose, nor do we further process this information.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, rent, or trade email addresses with third parties.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose email addresses if required by law or if necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

Data Security

Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of the collected email addresses.

Data Breach Protocol: In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will take immediate steps to mitigate any harm and will notify affected users as required by law.

User Rights

Opt-out and Removal: Users can opt out of receiving communications from us or request the deletion of their email address from our records.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

No Cookies: We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies for collecting personal information on our platform.

Policy Updates

Changes to Policy: We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the platform.

Notification of Changes: Users are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy for any updates.

Queries and Complaints

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our handling of email addresses, please contact us at privacy@ecoloader.com.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 26/08/2025.
